The Process

Five simple steps, start to finish.

Most results land in 5–10 days — from the moment you order to a report you can actually read.

  1. 1

    Order your kit

    Ships in 1 business day · 2–4 days to arrive

    Pick a panel or take the quiz. Your kit ships fast with sample bottles, a cold pack, gloves, and everything you need to collect cleanly.

  2. 2

    Scan the QR code

    ~1 minute

    Scan the insert to register your sample. That starts a digital, tamper-evident chain-of-custody — no paper forms, no illegible handwriting.

  3. 3

    Collect at home

    ~10 min water · 5–7 days passive air

    Follow the illustrated instructions. Water takes minutes to draw and seal; passive air badges simply sit in the room for a few days.

  4. 4

    Drop at UPS

    Same-day shipping

    Seal it in the prepaid mailer with the frozen gel pack and hand it to UPS. Real-time tracking follows your sample all the way to the bench.

  5. 5

    Get your report

    Read it in ~1 minute

    You receive lab-grade numbers compared to EPA limits, each with a plain-English interpretation and clear next steps.
