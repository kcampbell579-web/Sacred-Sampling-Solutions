Questions, answered

Everything you need to know.

How is this different from a hardware-store test strip?

Strips give you an approximate color read for one or two parameters. We run your sample on laboratory instruments (ICP-MS, GC-MS, LC-MS/MS) that quantify exact concentrations down to parts-per-trillion, then compare each result against EPA limits. It's the difference between a guess and a measurement.

How long does it take to get results?

Most reports are delivered in 5–7 business days from when your sample reaches the lab. You'll get a tracking update when it arrives and an email the moment your certified report is ready.

What exactly comes in the kit?

A premium box with laboratory-grade sample bottles, gloves, a pre-printed sample label, a QR card linking to step-by-step video instructions, a cold pack where required, and a prepaid return shipping label. Everything you need — nothing you don't.

Is the collection hard to do?

No. Most collections take under five minutes. Scan the QR card, follow the guided steps, seal the bottle, and drop the box in the mail. If you get stuck, our team is one message away.

Can I use these results for real estate or legal purposes?

The Comprehensive panel is processed under documented chain-of-custody conditions, which makes results defensible in real estate, compliance, and legal contexts. If chain-of-custody matters for your situation, choose Comprehensive.

Do you sell filters or treatment systems?

No — and that's intentional. We're independently operated with no financial ties to filter or remediation companies, so there's no incentive to over-report a problem. You get the data; what you do next is up to you.
