FAQ

Questions, answered.

How is this different from a hardware-store test strip?

Strips give a rough color estimate for one or two parameters. We ship your actual sample to an accredited lab where it's measured on professional instruments (ICP-MS, GC-MS, LC-MS/MS) and reported as a precise number compared to EPA limits.

How long do results take?

Most reports are ready in 5–7 business days from when your sample reaches the lab. You'll get a clear digital report by email.

Is shipping really free?

Yes — every kit ships free to you and includes a prepaid return mailer, so there's no cost to send your sample back.

What is chain-of-custody and why does it matter?

Chain-of-custody is documented, traceable handling of your sample from collection to analysis. It's the standard used for legally defensible results, and it comes on every kit.

Do you sell filters or treatment systems?

No. We're independently operated with no treatment products to sell, so there's no conflict of interest riding on your results.
